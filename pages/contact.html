Frequently Asked Questions

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). Free shipping is available on orders over ₹5,000. Track your order anytime using the tracking number sent to your email.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Orders' section.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Some items like electronics have a 15-day return window.

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our international shipping options in the near future.

You can reach us via email at support@shopmate.com, call us at +91 800 123 4567, or use the live chat feature on our website available 24/7.
