Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping are also available at checkout.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Returns are free for customers. International returns may have shipping fees.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay.

Do you offer gift wrapping? +

Yes! Gift wrapping is available for an additional charge. You can select this option at checkout and include a personalized message.
